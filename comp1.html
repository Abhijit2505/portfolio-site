In line with i4C’s vision to encourage technology-driven breakthroughs that make life simpler, thi blogathon has encouraged students across India to create and foster a culture of innovative thinking. i4C Blogathon is a technical blog-writing competition that allowed students to explore 3 crucial topics.
In this competiton I have narrated a new method based of Machine Intelligence that can help us in renewable energy generation and also won the "Consolation Prize".
As a call of the century, renewable energy is one of the utmost necessity for the upcoming days, I have designed an idea for a system that will be controlled by Machine Intelligence and can use "Human Body Temperature" and the "Radiation Phenomenon" as two important pillars of the generating system.
