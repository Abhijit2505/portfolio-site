Our heroes don’t wear capes, they wear masks and white coats; our heroes don’t fight monsters, they fight pandemics.
Even with millions in our accounts, we are incapable of paying for the kind of work they have been doing in this tough time. A salute, a clap, a few words of appreciation might not make much difference to you but it's sure to make a difference in the lives of those providing us essential services. I have ideated a long term innovation project "The Frontline Warriors" for a social cause of recognizing the hard work the corona warriors have performed.
The aim of our initiative is to provide the much needed recognition to our frontline warriors for their work and bring them into limelight of the society.
